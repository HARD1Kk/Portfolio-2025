Project Overview

Auralify is a music streaming web application that allows users to listen to their favorite songs, discover new music, and enjoy a personalized music experience. Users can create an account, upload their music collection, and listen to their favorite songs anytime, anywhere.

The application also provides a recommendation system that suggests songs based on the user's listening history and preferences. Users can create playlists, rate songs, and share their favorite music with friends.
