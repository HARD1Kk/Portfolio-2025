Live Link

Project Overview

Geng is a versatile virtual assistant designed to streamline your daily digital interactions. Leveraging the power of the Web Speech API for voice recognition and speech synthesis, Geng can respond to casual greetings, open popular websites like Google, YouTube, and Facebook, and answer your queries with commands such as "What is [topic]" and "Who is [person]." It can also tell jokes, provide the current time and date, play your favorite songs, open the calculator, and access Instagram directly.

Developed using JavaScript for its programming logic and HTML for the user interface, Geng efficiently manages tasks and enhances productivity, offering a convenient and engaging digital assistant experience.
